Prompt Library

Copy-paste these into Open WebUI. Tested with llama3.1:8b and qwen3:30b.

Education

"Explain [topic] like I'm 12 years old. Use analogies and simple words."

"Generate a 5-question quiz about [topic]. After I answer, give me feedback."

"Create an lesson plan for [subject] grade [X]. Include: objectives, activities, materials, assessment."

"Summarize this text in 3 bullet points a 10-year-old would understand: [paste text]"

"I'm a teacher. Suggest 3 differentiated activities for mixed-ability students learning [topic]."

Writing

"Help me write a persuasive essay about [topic]. Give me an outline with 3 main arguments."

"Rewrite this paragraph to be more concise and professional: [paste text]"

"Generate 5 creative writing prompts for high school students."

"Check this email for tone and clarity. Suggest improvements: [paste email]"

Language Learning

"Translate this Chinese text to English, then explain any idioms or cultural references: [paste text]"

"I'm learning Chinese. Break down this sentence word by word: [paste sentence]"

"Generate a dialogue between a tourist and a hotel receptionist in [language]. Include 10 vocabulary words."

"Explain the difference between [word A] and [word B] with examples."

Technical

"I'm stuck on this math problem: [paste]. Walk me through step by step without giving the answer."

"Explain how [technology] works. Use analogies. Target audience: high school students."

"Debug this code. Explain what's wrong and why: [paste code]"

"Generate a Python script that [task]. Include comments explaining each step."
