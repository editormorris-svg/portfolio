- [ ] **Safeguarding Requirements**

- [ ] **Data Protection Principles**
  - Is data minimization addressed?

- [ ] **Parent Communication Standards**

- [ ] **Clarity & Specificity**

  - Are procedures step-by-step?
  - Are exceptions addressed?
