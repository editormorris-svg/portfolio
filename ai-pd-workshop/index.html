Best Practices & Ethics

✓ DO

  • Always review AI-generated content before use
  • Verify facts and add your expertise
  • Disclose AI use to students appropriately
  • Maintain student privacy and data security
  • Use AI to enhance, not replace, teaching
  • Encourage critical thinking about AI outputs

✗ DON'T

  • Use AI outputs without review or editing
  • Share sensitive student data with AI tools
  • Rely solely on AI for grading high-stakes work
  • Ignore bias or errors in AI-generated content
  • Use AI to replace human connection
  • Forget to cite AI assistance when required
