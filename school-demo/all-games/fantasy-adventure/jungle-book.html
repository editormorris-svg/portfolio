Welcome to the Jungle!

The moon rises over the Seeonee jungle. You are Mowgli, a human cub raised by wolves. The monsoon season approaches, and Shere Khan the tiger has returned to the jungle.

Bagheera the panther has warned you: you must prove yourself worthy of the jungle's protection, or return to the man-village forever.
