Rajah's Diamond

Robert Louis Stevenson - An Interactive Mystery for EAL Learners

Bank of Hindustan, Calcutta, 1875

You are John E.R. Scott, a junior clerk at the Bank of Hindustan. Your life is one of quiet routine until the day the Rajah of Bhithoria deposits his family treasure - a magnificent diamond known as "The Diamond of the Rajahs."

When the diamond vanishes from the vault, you become the prime suspect. Your career, reputation, and freedom hang in the balance. You must investigate, gather clues, and find the real thief before it's too late!

"Three days and a half, and the sun had turned; the diamond was gone; and I was shut up in this great city like a thief."
