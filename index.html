Career Highlights

25 years across cultures — Thailand, UAE, Hong Kong, and beyond

The Leap • December 2024

When "Career Suicide" Was the Only Option

When I left Wellington College Hangzhou in December 2024, many—including myself—thought I was committing career suicide. In China, breaking your contract is something you simply don't do. No proper reference letter. A black mark that makes the next job nearly impossible. Complicated work permit applications where you must explain the gap, the departure, the unspoken taboo.

But I knew I had to make a change. My elderly mother needed me. We shipped our entire life to Thailand, found a community where she felt safe and comfortable, and I took a breath for the first time in years.

A maternity cover in the UK followed—April to August 2025—while I interviewed for something better. I wanted to be closer to Mom but didn't know where in Asia would give me the balance I needed: life, work, and play.

"I am eternally grateful to NAS Jiaxing for giving me this opportunity to start over. To become part of this loving, supportive community. To finally call somewhere home."
