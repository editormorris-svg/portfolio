You need: - A Chinese company to sponsor you, OR - Your school to apply under their entity, OR - A WFOE (Wholly Foreign-Owned Enterprise) — expensive and overkill for a teacher **Also Required:** PSB filing (公安备案) within 30 days of ICP approval. Extra paperwork. --- ## The Two-Site Strategy (Recommended) Don't move 0604.ai. **Keep both.** | Site | Hosting | Content | Audience | ICP Needed | |------|---------|---------|----------|------------| | **0604.ai** | GitHub Pages (US) | Everything — personal, journals, VPN guides, full agent logs | You, global audience, unrestricted | ❌ No | | **nasjx.ai** (example) | China VPS or school server | Professional AI education only — lesson plans, demo guides, student tools | School PD, colleagues, students, China network | ✅ Yes | **Why two sites:** 1.
